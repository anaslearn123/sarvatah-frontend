Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Sarvatah ("we", "us", "our") operates this website to provide information about our IT training courses and to allow prospective students to enquire, subscribe to updates, and enroll. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

When you fill out a contact form, subscribe to our newsletter, download a course syllabus, or enroll in a course, we may collect:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • The course(s) you are interested in
  • Any message or details you submit through our forms
  • Basic page-visit analytics (page viewed, referrer) to understand site usage

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide course information
  • Send you the course syllabus or brochure you requested
  • Send occasional newsletters and updates, if you subscribe
  • Process course enrollment and payment
  • Improve our website and course offerings

3. How We Protect Your Information

Your information is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized Sarvatah staff. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Your Choices

You may unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the link in any email we send, or by contacting us directly. You may also request that we delete your personal information by contacting us at the details on our Contact page.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact page or WhatsApp us at +91 70582 34395.
